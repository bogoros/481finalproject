Instructions

To build this project you must do the following:

1. Open and download all files from the zip file for this project. 
2. Open the file "index.html" in your preferred browser. (only important step)
2. From the browser you can click and navigate the project from the home page.
3. From the home page you can either scroll down or click on the different pages in the upper right corner.
3. When done just close the page in the browser.
