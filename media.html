New Music Release


     Bogdan Oros' latest music release is a powerful expression of faith and love in Jesus Christ. The new single, "You Still Fight", tells the story of a relentless and unwavering love that never gives up, even in the face of adversity. The lyrics speak of a love that fights for us, guides us, and ultimately saves us.

     The album, "Trust In You", continues this theme of unwavering faith and trust in Jesus. Through a collection of powerful and heartfelt songs, Bogdan invites listeners to put their trust in Jesus and experience the love and grace that he offers. Each song is a testament to the transformative power of Jesus' love, and a reminder that even in the midst of our struggles and doubts, we can find hope and peace in him.
